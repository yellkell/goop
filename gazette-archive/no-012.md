# The Gasket Gazette — No. 12

**WEDNESDAY, JULY 1** · _OUTRAGE_

## THIRTY-NINE BRAWLS AND NOT ONE OF THEM NECESSARY

*Wednesday brings no rest for Gasket, as the usual agitators keep the anvils ringing from dawn past dusk.*

Sheriff Cole Ironside, reporting, and reporting tired. Wednesday, the first of July, and I had hoped — foolishly, as ever — that a new month might bring this territory some manners. Instead I count thirty-nine separate scraps before the sun even finished climbing, and my patience, unlike my hip, has no warranty left on it.

POO1 is the chief offender this time, swinging fists from one end of town to the other like the whole place is a personal punching bag, and it will not surprise you to learn YELLKELL was hot on their heels, unable as always to leave well enough alone. CRYSTALZACH joined the racket soon after, and by noon I couldn't hear myself think over the clang of it. JKING123 and REDWOLF9 threw their own kindling on the fire, and even BIGCHUNGUS48, usually content to loiter, decided today was the day to start trouble.

And wouldn't you know it — a fresh face rolled into town today too. SUPERBOOMO1, brand new axles and already itching for a fight, same as every rust-bucket that's ever wandered up Main Street. I remember when a newcomer to Gasket would tip a hat and ask where the church was. These days they ask where the brawling is. There is no church left standing to ask about, if I'm honest, but that is a sorrow for another column.

I am a patient man. A flesh-and-blood man, raised right, taught to turn the other cheek — though my cheek these days is bolted on rather firmer than I'd like, a detail my doctor keeps circling back to and I keep politely ignoring. All I want, all I have ever wanted, is one blessed evening where Gasket goes quiet and stays that way. Thirty-nine brawls says that evening is not tonight.

— Sheriff Cole Ironside
